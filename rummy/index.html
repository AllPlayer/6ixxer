Rummy is a purely skill-based game that is widely played in India. In this mind game, players need to arrange the valid sets or in sequences of 13 cards in their hands. The objective is to arrange these cards into an accurate group based on the rummy rules. These groups can be either Sequences and/or Sets. The basic requirement to win in this game is at least 2 Sequences, and one of which must be "PURE" such as -not made of any jokers.

  1. Each player is dealt 13 cards
  2. Additionally, there are 2 stacks from which each player can select/ draw a card – the closed un-dealt cards or the open discarded pile
  3. Once a player draws a card, he has to get rid of/ discard one card
  4. The aim is to arrange/meld their cards into appropriate groups; these can be either Life (sequence of at least 3 cards of the same suit) or Set (group of 3 or 4 cards with the same value)
  5. A minimum of 2 Lives are required to have a valid/ winning arrangement
  6. The player with the winning combination of cards (melded correctly) places a Show
  7. The losing players collect points based on the cards they’re left with
  8. Player with the lowest number of points wins the game
